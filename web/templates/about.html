Our History

As everything great has a beginning, a visionary educator and a great pioneer of education our Founding Father Fr.schenidon shared his dream to create a sanctuary of learning,a place where young minds could soar to new heights and shape a brighter future.


Thus the story of Sts.Peter and paul Nursery school began in 1978 and officially opened on Monday 22nd May the same year.First school management entailed of Sr.Joanna whiteMore as the headteacher and Mrs.Mbugua as.teaching staff. The school began with five pupils;Kelvin Njonge,Njambi,Ann Wanjiru,Wairimu and Wakenyi children to the late philip kamau chairman to building committee of the school.


First recorded class had 9 pupils and later recorded an increase to 44 pupils the same year.The non teaching staff then haf only one cook,Mary.


In 20th june 1978 the first open day was held and parents expressed gratitude and appreciation for what the school fraternity had achieved.In 1979 the school began a full attendance program and in May the same year the late Sr.Angelina Khayeka a Loreto Sister joined the school.In 1979 almost all the pupils haf been accepted into Primary school.


In january 1980 Mrs. Teresia Kamau joined the staff and on 3rd December the same year first committee meeting was held and Sr.Angelina Khayeka appointed as the Headteacher after Sr.joanna whiteMore was transferred to Loreto Msongari.


In 1986 Sr. Angelina.wemt abroad for futher studies and Sr.Justina Nthambi from Loreto sisters appointed the Headteacher. On 19th march 1986 the first mass was celebrated by Fr.Michael Mungai. In 1990 Fidelis Nyakio was appointed headteacher in place of Sr Justina.


In 1999 Tr Teresia Kamau was appointed headteacher and Tr.Fidelis left the school. In 2000 Fr.Simon kamomoe and Tr.Teresia founded Sts. Peter and Paul primary school which began with fifteen children in Nursery and later progressed to Primary school(Kangoya) in 2002.


Through God and a supportive management system we have recorded a series of improvement in academic performance with over one thousand students and counting who beleive in Sts Peter and Paul as the best provider of quality education.Modern facilities are among our top priorities to spearhead our vision.


The continuation of high motivation,hardwork and discipline aspire us to grow to even greater heights and to be the best high quality education providers which will be evident in the increasing number of learners to i still them with longlife knowledge as God intends.
